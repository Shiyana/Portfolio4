Process

Step 3: Used Elbow and Silhouette methods to determine the optimal number of clusters (k).

Challenges & Solutions

  • Unclear cluster boundaries: Mitigated by trying multiple values of k and using Silhouette Score for validation.
  • Presence of outliers: Used statistical filtering with IQR and z-scores before training.
  • Interpretability of clusters: Examined feature means per cluster and created labels accordingly (e.g., High Income-Low Spend group).
